The song Kabar Ma Kyay Bu translates to “Until the end of the world”, and became emblematic of the 1988 protests. Adapted from the original Kansas classic Dust in the Wind (1977), the lyrics captures a sense of hope for democracy.

A Yay Kyee Pe, which means "It's important now", is one of the masterpiece songs by Mun Awng.

Lu Htu Tay Tan is the Burmese cover of Do You Hear The People Sing? from the 1980 musical Les Misérables.
